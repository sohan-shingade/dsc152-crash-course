Dataset: fitness

A health research team is studying factors associated with cardiovascular fitness. They collect data from a random sample of 400 adults enrolled in various exercise programs. The resulting dataset, called fitness, contains the following columns:

  • participant_id (double): unique identifier
  • age (double): age in years
  • exercise_hours (double): weekly hours of exercise
  • program (character): type of exercise program ("cardio", "strength", "mixed")
  • resting_hr (double): resting heart rate in bpm
  • vo2_max (double): maximum oxygen consumption (mL/kg/min), a measure of cardiovascular fitness
  • improved (double): 1 if the participant showed clinically significant improvement after 6 months, 0 otherwise

Assume all categorical variables are already coded as factors in R with the following reference levels: program = "cardio".
